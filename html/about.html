Simple Introduction about image
This is a wall painting which can be found at the stairway to the cross-over of the trains on Gainsborough St.
In the image, a little boy is painted by the black and white colors on the wall. A few red colors also been used in the painting. He carried some papers or newspapers and held a can of red spray paint.
Besides, a slogan called "I love Boston" is painted on the top of the boy.
